500/500 — Rules & Rationale

Why: I lean on AI too much. This is a constraint to grow my own voice.

Rules

  • 500 words/day minimum. Format flexible.
  • No AI drafting. Only spelling/grammar fixes allowed.
  • Public log. Each day gets a page in the Archive.
  • Influence ledger. Note who/what shaped the piece.
  • Exceptions log. If I bend a rule, I say how/why.
  • Missed day? $5 to charity and continue.
